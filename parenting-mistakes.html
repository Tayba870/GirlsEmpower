10 Mistakes Even Great Parents Make With Teens

May 5, 2023 8 min read

Parenting teenagers can feel like navigating uncharted waters. Even the most well-intentioned parents can fall into common traps that may inadvertently strain their relationship with their teens. Here are 10 common mistakes and how to avoid them:

1. Solving Problems Too Quickly

When teens share problems, our instinct is to immediately offer solutions. However, what they often need most is someone to listen without judgment. Practice active listening by reflecting back what you hear ("It sounds like you're feeling...") before offering advice.

2. Taking Moodiness Personally

Teen mood swings are more about their developing brains than about you. Instead of reacting to their tone, acknowledge their feelings ("You seem really upset about this") while setting boundaries about respectful communication.

3. Oversharing on Social Media

Posting about your teen's achievements or struggles without their permission can feel like a violation of privacy. Always ask before sharing anything about them online, and respect their boundaries.
