Why Mindfulness in the Classroom?

Mindfulness practices help children stay present, regulate emotions, and build empathy. These short exercises can be done daily to support well-being and learning readiness.

Sample Activities

  • Breathing Buddy: Students lie down with a stuffed animal on their stomachs and watch it rise and fall as they breathe.
  • 5-Minute Meditation: Guided meditation focusing on the senses and grounding techniques.
  • Gratitude Circle: Each student shares one thing they're grateful for that day.

Teacher Tip

Start each day with a 5 to 6 minute mindfulness activity. Consistency builds habit and helps students transition into learning mode calmly.
