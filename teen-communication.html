Getting Your Teen to Actually Talk to You

April 8, 2023 7 min read

Communication with teens often feels like pulling teeth. These research-backed techniques can help create more open dialogues with your adolescent.

1. The Car Conversation Trick

Teens often open up more when they don't have to make eye contact. Try having important conversations during car rides or while doing an activity together.

2. The 10-Minute Rule

When your teen initiates conversation, give them your full attention for at least 10 minutes without multitasking. This builds trust for bigger conversations later.
