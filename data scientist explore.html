What Data Scientists Do

Data scientists analyze and interpret complex data to help organizations make strategic decisions. They combine expertise in statistics, programming, and machine learning to uncover hidden patterns, predict trends, and solve critical business problems.

Key Skills Needed:

Python/R Programming Statistics & Probability Machine Learning Data Visualization SQL Databases Data Wrangling Big Data Technologies Business Acumen
