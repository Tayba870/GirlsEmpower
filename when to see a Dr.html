When to See a Doctor

While every girl body is different, here are signs to look out for:

  • Your period has not started by age 15
  • You have not had a period in 3+ months
  • Your cramps are so bad you miss school or activities
  • You bleed through pads/tampons every hour
  • You feel very depressed or anxious around your period

Do not suffer in silence help is available.
