Common Questions

What is GirlEmpower+?
GirlEmpower+ is an Ethiopian-led initiative focused on supporting girls and women's mental health through resources, community, and education. We believe every girl deserves to feel strong, seen, and supported.
Who can join the GirlEmpower+ community?
Any girl, woman, educator, parent, or ally who supports mental wellness and empowerment especially for young women in Ethiopia and beyond.
Are the resources free?
Yes! Most of our articles, guides, and support tools are completely free. Some premium products help fund our mission but are always optional.
How do I connect with a therapist?
Visit our "Therapists & Support" page to find licensed professionals and peer groups in your area or online.
Can I share my story anonymously?
Absolutely. Many women choose to share anonymously to protect their privacy while inspiring others.
Do you offer support in Amharic?
Yes! We are committed to offering key resources and support in Amharic and plan to expand into other local languages soon.
How can teachers use your resources?
Our Parent & Teacher Resource section includes classroom strategies, emotional regulation tools, and trauma-informed practices designed for educators in Ethiopia and beyond.
Is everything online?
Most of our tools are digital so they are accessible from anywhere. But we also partner with local organizations to provide offline support when possible.
How do I ask a question not listed here?
You can reach out directly via email at taybaali870@gmail.com or visit our Contact Us page.
Why focus on Ethiopian girls and women?
Because we believe healing starts with cultural understanding. As feminists and advocates, we want to build a future where Ethiopian girls grow up knowing their worth without shame or silence.
