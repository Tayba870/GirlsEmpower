Managing PMS & Cramps

It is common to feel cramps, mood swings, or tiredness before or during your period. These symptoms are called PMS (Premenstrual Syndrome).

Try these tips to feel better:

  • Drink warm tea or water with lemon
  • Apply a heating pad on your belly
  • Do light stretching or yoga
  • Get enough rest and eat healthy snacks
