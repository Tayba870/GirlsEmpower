Recognizing Common Signs

Young women may show these subtle indicators of mental health challenges:

  • Changes in sleep or eating patterns lasting 2+ weeks
  • Withdrawal from social activities they previously enjoyed
  • Decline in academic performance without clear cause
  • Increased irritability or emotional outbursts
  • Expressions of hopelessness or excessive guilt

Starting the Conversation

Choose the Right Moment

Find a private, comfortable setting when you're both relaxed. Avoid times of high stress.

Use "I" Statements

"I've noticed you seem tired lately" rather than "You look terrible."

Normalize the Experience

"Lots of people go through tough times. How are you really doing?"
