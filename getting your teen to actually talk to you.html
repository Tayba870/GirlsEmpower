Getting Teens to Open Up

Communication strategies that actually work

Creating Safe Spaces

Teens talk when they feel safe, not when they're pressured.

✅ Do:

  • Talk side-by-side (car rides work great)
  • Keep reactions neutral
  • Respect their timing

❌ Don't:

  • Demand eye contact
  • Interrogate after school
  • Overreact to small disclosures

Unexpected Conversation Starters

  • "What's something funny you saw online today?"
  • "If you could design the perfect day, what would it include?"
  • "What's something you know now that you wish you knew a year ago?"
