Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

The menstrual cycle is a natural process that prepares your body for pregnancy each month. It involves changes in hormone levels and the shedding of the uterine lining during your period. Understanding your cycle can help you feel more in control and make informed decisions about your health.

Most cycles last between 21 and 35 days, with bleeding lasting around 3 to 7 days. Every girls cycle is unique so take time to learn what is normal for you!
