The Digital Parenting Handbook

April 22, 2023 10 min read

In today's digital world, parenting requires new strategies to keep teens safe online while respecting their growing independence. This guide covers essential approaches to digital parenting.

1. Start With Education, Not Fear

Instead of just listing dangers, teach your teen how to evaluate online content critically. Discuss concepts like digital footprints, privacy settings, and recognizing misinformation.

2. Create a Family Media Agreement

A collaborative agreement about device usage times, appropriate content, and online behavior works better than imposed rules. Include both parent and teen commitments.
