Pain Management Tips

If your cramps hurt a lot, try these safe ways to feel better:

  • Use a hot water bottle or heating pad
  • Take a warm bath
  • Try gentle stretches or yoga
  • Drink herbal tea (like chamomile)
  • Ask a parent or doctor about painkillers like ibuprofen

Never ignore severe pain talk to a trusted adult or healthcare provider.
