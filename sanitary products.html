Types of Sanitary Products

There are many options to choose from:

  • Pads: Stick on your underwear and absorb the flow.
  • Tampons: Inserted into the vagina to absorb flow inside the body.
  • Menstrual Cups: Reusable silicone cups placed inside the vagina.
  • Period Underwear: Absorbent underwear worn alone or with other products.

Try different types to see what feels most comfortable for you.
