Phases of the Menstrual Cycle

The menstrual cycle has four main phases:

  • Menses Phase: This is your period when the lining of your uterus sheds.
  • Follicular Phase: Your ovaries prepare an egg for release.
  • Ovulation: One egg is released from the ovary.
  • Luteal Phase: If the egg is not fertilized, hormone levels drop, and your next period begins.

Knowing these phases helps you understand your body better and track any irregularities.
